ZorroMC

Where The Fox Builds

ZorroMC is a 1.17.1 Java Minecraft server where we pride ourselves in giving a feature-rich gameplay, without any of the fluff from Factions, Towny, McMMO, etc. Our server is centered around the community, thanks to the various events, polls, and projects we host. To ensure that our community remains mature, active, and well-behaved, we require you to fill an application in order to get into the server. This also helps in blocking the vast majority of troublemakers. However, we try to be as inclusive as possible, so we'll accept anyone so long as you do your upmost on the application and demonstrate that you are a mature human being.

Our key values

  • Quality is about making sure our server is high-quality in every way possible.
  • Every feature we implement is only done so because it meets our standards of a semi-vanilla SMP and has been well-tested.
  • We have a high degree of organization, making sure everything is as efficient as possible.
  • Staff members are trained and only added if they met our high standards.
  • Democracy is all about giving the players choice on the server.
  • Every feature, change, or decision must be vetted by the community before they come into effect.
  • For a feature to be implemented, a large enough majority must vote and agree to it.
  • Staff will never push a feature onto the server that nobody asked for.
  • Transparency is about keeping our members updated with everything that goes on behind the scenes.
  • We disclose whenever we made a change to the server as simple as a plugin update or a minor configuration tweak.
  • We will also disclose if a member has been banned, a staff has been removed, or if a severe enough situation occurs.
  • We also promise to be transparent in terms of how finances are handled, with a monthly report showcasing how much we are earning, how much was spent on the server or plugins, if we met our goal or not, etc.
  • Cooperation is about making sure our members can get together to accomplish great things.
  • We plan projects for various areas like the spawn and shopping district, and members can too form their own projects with others.
  • Both the staff team and community members can setup events, so we can all have a good time.
  • We don't require you to participate in projects or events, but we highly encourage you to.

Our top features

We utilize the William Wyther's Overhauled Overworld datapack in conjunction with the Caves and Cliffs addon for amazing terrain generation. All biomes have been enhanced in their own unique way, mountains reach heights never seen before, caves have been expanded, and the world height is now 384 blocks tall, making for an incredible experience when exploring.

Our online map has a vanilla-like style and is designed for navigation first, no fancy intensive 3D mode or unnecessary features. Players can set markers with the permission of a staff member via a sign or banner.

Our Discord bot comes with many integrations with the server, including:
  • Bi-directional chat between Minecraft and Discord.
  • Proximity voice chat
  • Role sync between Minecraft and Discord
  • Nickname sync between Minecraft and Discord (for Foxy role only)

Once crafted, consuming the chorus fruit will teleport you to your bed from anywhere in the world. See the Custom recipes tab for the full recipe.

Taken from the 1.18 snapshots, this feature will reduce the amount of torches needed to light up an area. While this may affect the spawn rate of some farms, we believe the trade off will be worth it as we're simply backporting a feature from a later update.

Once all logs of a tree are chopped off, the leaves will instantly decay on their own. No more waiting around for leaves to decay or removing them manually, they just disappear™.

Right click the shulker box while holding it, and it will open the contents of the box without having to place it. Incredibly convenient for use as a backpack or quick access to some key items.

We have a wide variety of custom recipes, taken from the vanillatweaks.net website save for the enchanted chorus fruit.

Our events

Community builds

A big part of the server, community builds are where we all agree to plan and build out something in particular. While the primary focus is on spawn, other projects can be tackled elsewhere such as the nether hub and a rail network. We may also use the Litematica mod to help facilitate the building process as a group.

Building challenges

Participate in building challenges to win large prizes. Sent out biweekly, you first pay an entry fee, then build something according to the given topic. Topics can be anything, from seasonal themes to pixel art or even one-chunk challenges. At the end of the contest staff will decide who's the winner.

Minigames

We host minigames events for players that would like to relax and have some fun. These events are done at least once a week and can include build battles, tag, UHC, and various other multiplayer maps. There is also a role you can select to get pinged for each event announcement, so you can never miss one.

Our rules

General Rules
  1. Be mature and respectful to others
  2. No political or controversial arguments
  3. Don’t be a racist (No slurs)
  4. No inappropriate or explicit content
  5. No advertising or spamming
  6. Speak English publicly
Minecraft Rules
  1. Settle at least 300 blocks away from others
  2. No griefing or stealing
  3. No cheating, exploiting, or hacking (basic client mods are fine)
  4. Don’t cause lag by running massive farms or machines
  5. Avoid destructive pranks, repair any damage done
  6. No PvP without consent
Discord Rules
  1. Chat in the correct channels
  2. Utilize spoiler tags when appropriate
  3. No offensive status
  4. Keep your nickname as your Minecraft username
  5. Ping staff only when needed
  6. Open a ticket when you have an issue that needs solving
